# Problem 3 — Conservation of Energy

We have a pendulum released from a small angle.

## Step 1: Height drop

The pendulum falls slightly as it swings down.

This drop is small because the angle is only 15 degrees.

Height ≈ 0.034 m

## Step 2: Convert energy

At the top:
- energy is stored as height (potential energy)

At the bottom:
- all energy becomes motion (kinetic energy)

So the speed at the bottom comes from that height.

After calculation:

Speed ≈ 0.82 m/s

**Final answer:** 0.82 m/s
