# Problem 4 — Energy and Momentum

## Step 1: Speed before collision

The block slides down and converts height into speed.

Higher height → higher speed

After calculation:

Speed ≈ 7.67 m/s

## Step 2: After collision

The block sticks to another block.

This is an inelastic collision.

Momentum is conserved, but energy is not.

After calculation:

Final speed ≈ 1.92 m/s

**Final answer:** 1.92 m/s
