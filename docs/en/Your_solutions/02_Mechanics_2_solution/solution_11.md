# Problem 11 — Time-Dependent Force

Force changes with time.

## Step 1: Acceleration

Acceleration is force divided by mass.

So acceleration also changes with time.

## Step 2: Velocity

Velocity is found by adding up (integrating) acceleration over time.

So velocity becomes a curved (non-linear) function.

## Step 3: Position

Position is found by integrating velocity.

So position becomes even more curved.

## Interpretation

- Motion is not uniform
- The path becomes increasingly complex over time

**Final idea:**  
Time-dependent forces create non-uniform, curved motion.
