# Problem 9 — Vertical Throw with Drag

There are two forces:

- gravity (downward)
- air resistance (opposes motion)

## What happens?

- Object slows down faster than normal
- It reaches a lower height than without air

## Maximum height

Smaller than the no-drag case

## Comparison

Without drag:
- motion is symmetric
- higher maximum height

With drag:
- motion loses energy
- height is reduced

**Final idea:**  
Air resistance reduces both speed and maximum height.
