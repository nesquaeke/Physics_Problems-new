# Problem 10 — Force Field and Power

## Velocity

Velocity comes from how position changes.

Result:

Velocity changes with time and is different in each direction.

## Momentum

Momentum = mass × velocity

Since mass is constant, momentum follows velocity.

## Acceleration

Acceleration is the change of velocity.

It is not constant here.

## Force

Force = mass × acceleration

So force also changes with time.

## Power

Power tells us how fast energy is transferred.

It depends on both:
- force
- velocity

Result:

Power increases strongly over time.

**Final idea:**  
Everything (velocity, force, power) depends on time and grows as time increases.
