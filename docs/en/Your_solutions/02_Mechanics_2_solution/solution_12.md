# Problem 12 — Work and Energy with Constant Force

## Step 1: Acceleration

Force is constant → acceleration is constant.

Acceleration = (3, 1)

## Step 2: Velocity

Velocity increases linearly with time.

## Step 3: Position

Position follows a curved path (parabola).

## Step 4: Work

At time = 3 s:

Work ≈ 102 J

## Step 5: Energy check

Change in kinetic energy = 102 J

Matches the work.

## Interpretation

This confirms:

Work = change in energy

**Final answer:**  
Work-energy theorem is verified
