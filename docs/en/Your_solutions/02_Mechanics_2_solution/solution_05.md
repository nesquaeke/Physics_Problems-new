# Problem 5 — Inelastic Collision

## Step 1: Final speed

A runner jumps onto a cart and they move together.

Momentum must stay the same.

After calculation:

Final speed = 1.0 m/s

**Final answer:** 1.0 m/s

---

## Step 2: Is energy conserved?

Before collision:
- energy = 315 J

After collision:
- energy = 105 J

Energy decreased.

## Why?

Because this is an inelastic collision:
- some energy becomes heat, sound, deformation

**Final answer:**  
Energy is NOT conserved
