# Problem 8 — Work of a Variable Force

This force behaves like a spring.

## Key ideas:

- The motion is oscillatory (back and forth)
- The force always pulls toward equilibrium

## Work

The work done depends on displacement.

Result:

Work = negative value proportional to distance squared

## Potential energy

Energy stored in the system increases with distance squared.

## Interpretation

- Force pulls inward
- Energy is minimum at the center

**Final idea:**  
This is standard spring (harmonic) motion.
