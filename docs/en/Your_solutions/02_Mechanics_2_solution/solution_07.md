# Problem 7 — Dynamics with Friction

We have two friction forces acting against motion:

1. Between the two blocks  
2. Between the bottom block and the floor  

## Step 1: Friction from top block

≈ 9.81 N

## Step 2: Friction from floor

≈ 29.43 N

## Step 3: Net force

Applied force = 45 N

Net force:

45 − 9.81 − 29.43 ≈ 5.76 N

## Step 4: Acceleration

Acceleration = force / mass

≈ 0.58 m/s²

**Final answer:** 0.58 m/s²
