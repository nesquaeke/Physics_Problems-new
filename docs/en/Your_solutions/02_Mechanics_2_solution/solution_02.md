# Problem 2 — Harmonic Motion

We are given an oscillating motion.

From the equation, we can read:

- amplitude = 0.2 m
- oscillation speed (angular frequency) = 10π

Mass = 10 kg

## 2.1 Spring constant

The stiffness of the spring depends on:
- mass
- how fast it oscillates

Faster oscillation → stiffer spring

After calculation:

Spring constant ≈ 9870 N/m

**Final answer:** 9870 N/m

---

## 2.2 Total energy

The total energy depends on:
- spring stiffness
- amplitude

Bigger amplitude → more energy

After calculation:

Energy ≈ 197 J

**Final answer:** 197 J
