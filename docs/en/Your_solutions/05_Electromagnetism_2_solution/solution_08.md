

## 8. EM Spectrum
**The Problem:** Put these types of light in order from the smallest wavelength to the largest wavelength.

**Explanation:**
Think of wavelength like a snake.
*   **Short wavelength** = High energy, very fast tight wiggles. Dangerous!
*   **Long wavelength** = Low energy, slow lazy wiggles. Safe!

**The Order (Increasing wavelength):**
1.  **Gamma rays** (Shortest wavelength, most dangerous, radioactive)
2.  **X-rays** (Looking at bones)
3.  **Ultraviolet** (Causes sunburns)
4.  **Infrared** (Night vision, body heat)
5.  **Microwaves** (Heating food)
6.  **Radio waves** (Longest wavelength, music in your car, completely safe)
