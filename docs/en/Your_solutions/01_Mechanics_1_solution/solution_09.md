## 9) Momentum Comparison

Momentum is:

`p = mv`

### Fly

Mass of fly:

`2 g = 0.002 kg`

Speed:

`10 m/s`

So:

`p_fly = 0.002 * 10 = 0.02 kg*m/s`

### Tennis ball

Mass of tennis ball:

`60 g = 0.060 kg`

Speed:

`1 m/s`

So:

`p_ball = 0.060 * 1 = 0.060 kg*m/s`

### Compare

`0.060 > 0.020`

So the tennis ball has greater momentum.

**Final answer:**  
The `60 g` tennis ball has greater momentum.

- Fly momentum: `0.02 kg*m/s`
- Tennis ball momentum: `0.06 kg*m/s`
