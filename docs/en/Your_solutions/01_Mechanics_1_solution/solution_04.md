## 4) Vector Calculus

Position is given by:

`r(t) = (3t^2)i + (5t - 8t^2)j`

We want velocity and acceleration.

### Step 1: Velocity

Velocity is the derivative of position:

`v(t) = dr/dt`

Differentiate each component:

- `d/dt(3t^2) = 6t`
- `d/dt(5t - 8t^2) = 5 - 16t`

So:

`v(t) = (6t)i + (5 - 16t)j`

**Final answer:**  
`v(t) = (6t, 5 - 16t)`

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

Acceleration is the derivative of velocity:

`a(t) = dv/dt`

Differentiate each component:

- `d/dt(6t) = 6`
- `d/dt(5 - 16t) = -16`

So:

`a(t) = 6i - 16j`

**Final answer:**  
`a(t) = (6, -16)`
